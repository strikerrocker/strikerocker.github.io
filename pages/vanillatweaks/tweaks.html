Sit on stairs and slabs

Sit on slabs and stairs by right clicking with an empty hand.

Sign editing

You can now right-click already placed signs to edit them.
Shift+right-clicking already placed signs clears them.

Mob Spawners can be silk touched

Mob Spawners drop themselves when you use silk touch.

Dragon eggs & Command blocks in creative tab

Command blocks can be found in the redstone creative tab.
Dragon eggs can be found in the decorations tab.

Buttons are named according to their material


Higher potion and enchanting levels

Potion and enchantment level roman numerals now are localized up to level 255.

Hoe Harvesting

Breaking grass or crops with a hoe will break a 3x3 of them. A Diamond Hoe will break a 5x5 instead.

Quick Armor Swapping

Right clicking an armor stand while sneaking will swap all armor quickly.

TNT placed next to lava will ignite itelf


Fire arrows set block on fire

Fire arrows when hit on block sets the block on fire.

Sponges can squished

Wet sponges on being squished set adjacent block as water if it's air.
